Say hello

Contact us

We would love to hear from you. There is no obligation and no fee — just a warm welcome. Tell us where you are and how you'd like to take part, and a volunteer will get back to you.

Our community in the Philippines is just getting started — a small circle of families and volunteers beginning to gather around the values of Sanskar (character), Seva (selfless service) and Sanghatan (unity).

So if there isn't a shakha (our weekly family gathering) listed near you yet, please don't let that stop you. Tell us where you are, and we'll do our best to connect you with others nearby so we can grow together. Whether you have a question, would like to bring your family along, or want to help start a gathering in your city — this is the place to reach us.

Drop us a line

Other ways to reach us

Find us & find your place

As our community is newly forming, some of these details are still being set up. We're publishing each one here as it is confirmed, so nothing below is a placeholder for long.

  • WhatsApp

  • Social media To be added

  • Where we meet

    We currently hold two regular gatherings, and everyone is welcome:

    • Every Sunday, 9:00–10:30 AM — our main all-ages shakha at the Hindu Temple, Paco, Manila.
    • Every Saturday, 5:30–7:00 PM — the Chhatrapati Shivaji Shakha for children and young people, at the Crimarcel Basketball Court.

    Message us on WhatsApp for exact times and directions. New to a city we're not in yet? Tell us where you are and help us begin one.

  • Our organiser (pracharak)

    Our main pracharak — a full-time volunteer organiser — looks after the gatherings and can help you take part. Reach them on WhatsApp above, or send the form; either way, a real volunteer replies.

  • What to expect when you reach out

    A real person — a fellow volunteer — will read your message and reply. There's no sales pitch and no obligation, just a friendly answer to your questions and, if you'd like, an invitation to join a gathering. Everyone who shares our values is welcome, whatever their background.

Where to find us

Our main shakha meets every Sunday, 9:00–10:30 AM at the Hindu Temple, Paco, Manila; the Chhatrapati Shivaji Shakha for children and youth meets every Saturday, 5:30–7:00 PM at the Crimarcel Basketball Court. For the exact meeting point and directions, message our pracharak on WhatsApp at +63 915 857 5603.

A community taking shape

HSS in the Philippines is a young, forming community — and it has begun. Two free weekly shakhas now meet in Metro Manila (see the details above), and in 2026 volunteers here also supported the Chant Mahaveer celebration in Manila and observed the International Day of Yoga in Makati. These are early, hopeful steps, with much more to come — and there is a place in the next chapter for you.
